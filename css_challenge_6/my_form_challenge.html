What if all guns disappeared?

Take the politics put of it. By the numbers, what would we gain - and lose - if all firearms suddenly were wiped off the face of the planet?

  • Approximately 500,000 people around the world are killed by guns each year. In terms of developed countries, the biggest losses are in the US, where citizens own 300 to 350 million guns in total.
  • More than 80% of attempted suicides with a gun end in death.
